I’m Ahamed Jishfaan M S, a professional Mechanical Engineer in Sharjah, UAE, specializing in mechanical drafting, project documentation, and process improvement. With years of experience in both technical and operational management, I bring a unique combination of expertise and a results-driven approach to every project. Professional Background In my recent role with Gtech Mulearn, I managed everything from technical drawings to project budgeting, consistently ensuring regulatory compliance and optimizing resource utilization. My background in mechanical engineering allows me to streamline processes, enhance productivity, and deliver successful outcomes for both government and private sector projects. I've also completed a research internship at KKEM, where I assisted with data analysis, literature reviews, and presented findings that contributed to innovative solutions for engineering challenges. These experiences have honed my skills in data-driven decision-making and engineering standards compliance, making me a versatile asset in any engineering project. Featured Projects and Achievements Throughout my career, I’ve taken on exciting projects that demonstrate my mechanical engineering skills and project management abilities: Rota Meter Calibration Apparatus: Designed and constructed a calibration tool that met industry standards and improved efficiency. Mobile-Controlled Pesticide Spraying Rover: A unique, eco-friendly project focused on precision and accessibility in agriculture. Award-Winning Performance: Recognized with the PM Foundation Award and placed at the Southern India Science Fair and Kerala Science Fair, celebrating innovation and dedication. Skills & Certifications I am proficient in AutoCAD, SolidWorks, and Revit, as well as MS Office Suite for project documentation. My certifications include ASNT NDT Level-2, HVAC Design using Revit 2021, and Oil & Gas Operations from Duke University. I am also trained in pipe hydraulics, thermal analysis, and mechanical drafting—skills that are essential for high-quality, accurate engineering work. Why Work with Me? As a professional mechanical engineer with a commitment to excellence, I aim to deliver efficient, innovative, and regulatory-compliant solutions to complex engineering challenges. My client-focused approach ensures clear communication, timely project delivery, and a dedication to meeting your unique requirements.
